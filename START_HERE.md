## ğŸ¯ Key Concepts (Must Know)

### 1. Order Blocks (OB)
- Last opposite candle before impulse
- Institutional unfilled orders
- High-probability reversal zones

### 2. Fair Value Gaps (FVG)
- Gaps between candle wicks
- Price imbalance areas
- Tend to get filled

### 3. Liquidity Zones
- Equal highs/lows
- Stop loss clusters
- Often swept before reversal

### 4. Break of Structure (BOS)
- Price breaks swing high/low
- Confirms trend change
- Trade pullbacks in BOS direction

### 5. TRAMA
- Adaptive moving average
- Responds to volatility
- Shows trend strength
